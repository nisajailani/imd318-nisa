All About Me (QURATUN NISA')

My name is Quratun Nisa' Binti Jailani, a 20-year-old girl, born on 31st March 2004 at Hospital Besar Kuala Lumpur. I have lived in Hulu Langat, Selangor my entire life with my parents and two other siblings. Me and my siblings grew up with our grandparents since we were litte. I was fortunate to grow up under the care of my grandparents, who looked after me from kindergarten to primary school while my parents were at work.
